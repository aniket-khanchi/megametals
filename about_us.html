About Us

Mega Metals is an IATF 16949 certified Manufacturer of
Aluminum Die Casting Components. We are a rapidly growing organization situated
in Khushkhera Industrial Area, one of the fastest growing industrial infrastructure
hubs of India. We have also gained cutting edge expertise in Aluminum Alloy
Components by supplying it for over 10 years to many Domestic and international
customers.

In the visionary guidance of our Managing Partner Mr. Raman Arora, our team of dedicated engineers and professionals always strive for excellence in all our activities.
Our resolve is to encourage & empower the people to outperform their
responsibilities and drive Mega Metals on the road of ever growing success.

Mission

To achieve and maintain leadership in our field with focus on Quality and Product Reliability, Environment & Safety through a participative work culture of transparency and ownership.  


Vision

To grow as a complete solution provider of Die Casting and Machining through socially responsible operations by providing products and services of superior quality to our customers. We also aim to have sustainable growth of our organization along with our employees and business associates.


Ideology

After being in this industry for 20 years, our founder Mr. Raman Arora’s ideology fundamentally revolves around providing Grade-A service to its customer, a safe and growing space for its committed workforce and a healthy
environment for its supply chain.

We believe in through thick and thin working culture and applying a very flexible system management. Our understanding, corporative staff and workers are the main pillars of strength.
  
Looking for long term association rather than short term gains is Our Management’s Prime Motto.
